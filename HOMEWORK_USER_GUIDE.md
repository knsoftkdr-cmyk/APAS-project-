# 📚 Student Homework - User Guide

## How to Use the Homework Feature

### Step 1: View Your Homework
When you navigate to the "Your Homework" section, you'll see a list of all pending homework assignments.

**What You'll See:**
- Homework title with subject and topic
- Number of questions (e.g., "5 Questions")
- Period number and assignment date
- Status badge: "Pending" (for incomplete) or "Submitted" (with green checkmark)

### Step 2: Start the Homework
Click the **"Start Homework"** button on any pending assignment.

⚠️ **Important**: Questions only appear AFTER you click "Start" - they're hidden in the list view to prevent accidental peeking.

---

## The Homework Attempt Screen

When you start a homework, you'll see:

### 1. ⏱️ Timer Banner at the Top
- **Large countdown display** showing time remaining (MM:SS format)
- **Timer Colors**:
  - 🟢 Green: More than 2 minutes left
  - 🟡 Yellow: 1-2 minutes left
  - 🔴 Red: Less than 1 minute left
  
- **Progress Counter**: Shows "X / Y Answered" (e.g., "3 / 5")
- **Auto-Submit Notice**: Your homework will submit automatically when time runs out

### 2. View Mode Buttons
Choose how to answer questions:
- **"One at a Time"**: See one question per screen with navigation buttons
- **"All Questions"**: See all questions at once on one scrollable page

You can switch between modes anytime without losing your answers.

---

## Two Ways to Answer

### Option A: One at a Time Mode (Recommended for Focus)
- See "Question X of Y" at the top
- Large text field for your answer
- **Previous** and **Next** buttons to navigate
- Shows how many questions you've answered in a badge

**Best for**: Staying focused on one question at a time

### Option B: All Questions Mode (Good for Review)
- All questions numbered and visible
- Answer all in one scrollable page
- Easier to compare questions
- See your overall progress

**Best for**: Quick review before submitting

---

## Submission

### 🟢 Manual Submit (Recommended)
1. Answer the questions at your own pace
2. Click **"Submit Homework"** button
3. Your homework is saved with a submission percentage:
   - If you answer 4 out of 5 questions = 80%
   - Unanswered questions count against you

⏰ The submit button works anytime you have time remaining.

### 🔄 Auto-Submit (When Time Runs Out)
- If the timer reaches **0:00**, your homework automatically submits
- Whatever answers you've entered are saved
- The submit button becomes disabled
- A final **"Submitted!"** message appears

### ⚠️ Warning Alert (Last 60 Seconds)
- A red alert box appears showing: "Your homework will auto-submit in MM:SS"
- You still have time to submit manually if preferred
- Final chance to complete remaining questions

---

## After Submission

✅ **You'll see**:
- Homework marked as "Submitted" with a green checkmark
- Your **submission percentage** (e.g., "80%")
- Date and time you submitted
- Your submitted answers displayed read-only

## Important Rules

| Rule | Details |
|------|---------|
| ⏱️ **5-Minute Timer** | All homework has exactly 5 minutes (300 seconds) |
| 🚫 **No Pausing** | Timer continues counting - can't pause |
| 💾 **Auto-Save** | Answers saved when you submit (manual or auto) |
| 🔒 **Read-Only After Submit** | Can't edit after submission (contact teacher if needed) |
| 📱 **Mobile Friendly** | Works on phones, tablets, and computers |

## Quick Tips

✨ **Pro Tips for Success:**
1. **Read all questions first** - Use "All Questions" mode to scan
2. **Answer easy ones first** - Build momentum and confidence
3. **Check the timer frequently** - The banner is always visible
4. **Use "One at a Time" for focus** - Minimize distractions
5. **Double-check before submitting** - Can't edit after submission
6. **Answer something if unsure** - 0% is worst case; partial answers better

## Troubleshooting

### ❓ "Questions aren't showing!"
- **Solution**: Click "Start Homework" button first. Questions only appear after you start.

### ❓ "Timer doesn't show!"
- **Solution**: Scroll to the top of the page. Timer is in the banner at the very top.

### ❓ "Can't click Submit button!"
- **Solution**: The button disables when time runs out (auto-submit happens instead). This is normal.

### ❓ "My answers disappeared!"
- **Solution**: Switch between view modes. Your answers are saved in both "One at a Time" and "All Questions" modes.

### ❓ "Submitted but didn't see confirmation!"
- **Solution**: Check if you see the green "Submitted ✓" badge. If not, contact your teacher.
